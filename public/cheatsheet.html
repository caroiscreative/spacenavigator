✦ Powerful combinations — features that shine together
G+X
Risk + Time Controls: open the risk panel and click a conjunction event to fly to it — then use the time scrubber to watch the near-miss approach happen in real time at any speed.
H+A
Density Map + Satellites: enable both layers and click an altitude band in the density panel. Every satellite in that orbital shell gets highlighted — instantly shows what's in the most crowded zones.
D+H
Debris + Density: see the red debris layer and the crowding heatmap simultaneously. The most powerful single view of the orbital environment problem.
4+B
Solar view + Asteroid Belt: zoom to the inner solar system and enable the belt. 600,000+ asteroids between Mars and Jupiter light up — their real orbital positions computed from MPC elements.
6+Y
Outer Solar + Interstellar: zoom to the outer solar system and enable the deep objects layer. ʻOumuamua, Borisov, and 3I/ATLAS appear with their actual hyperbolic trajectories crossing through the solar system.
T+X
Saturn + Time: fly to Saturn, enable time controls, and accelerate to ×10,000. Watch Saturn and its moons sweep through their orbits while the ring system catches the light.
5+F+C
Star sphere + Stars + Constellations: zoom to the star sphere and enable both the star field and constellation lines for the complete celestial sphere — 119,625 stars with all 86 classical patterns mapped.
